## Design Context

### Users
- Web3 builders, DAO contributors, hackathon participants, and DeFi users who need portable proof of credibility.
- Integrator dApps and protocol teams that need machine-readable reputation signals for gating and trust decisions.
- Typical context: user connects a Solana wallet, verifies aggregate credibility, and shares a portable passport identity across dApps.

### Brand Personality
- **Credible, composable, empowering**
- Emotional goals: confidence (trustworthy scoring), momentum (builders can progress faster), and fairness (less manual identity gatekeeping).

### Aesthetic Direction
- Product UI for daily operational use by crypto-native users; default dark theme is appropriate for usage context.
- Visual language should feel data-authentic and protocol-grade rather than speculative or decorative.
- Explicit anti-reference: generic AI dashboard tropes (template gradients, glassy card spam, weak hierarchy).

### Design Principles
1. **Portable trust first**: every screen should emphasize that reputation is unified and reusable across dApps.
2. **Explain scoring transparently**: make issuer authority weighting and score composition understandable at a glance.
3. **Actionable passport**: show concrete ways integrators can query and use the reputation passport.
4. **Accessible by default**: keyboard/focus support and WCAG-aware contrast are non-negotiable.
5. **Production over demo polish**: optimize for reliable data surfaces, clear states, and deployable architecture.
