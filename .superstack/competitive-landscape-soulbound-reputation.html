Soulbound Reputation Graph Protocol on Solana

Landscape date: 2026-05-08

Conclusion: no obvious project owns the full wedge of Solana-native SBT attestations + portable identity + zk reputation proofs + open reputation graph + Sybil-resistant scoring. The market is moderately crowded around adjacent primitives: Solana Attestation Service, Civic, Solana ID, Solid, Trusta, Range, Human Passport, World ID, Galxe, EAS, BrightID, and Polygon ID.

White Space

The strongest wedge is not to rebuild SAS. Build on SAS as the canonical attestation substrate, then specialize in open reputation graph computation, issuer/attestor weighting, zk score proofs, and developer APIs for apps that need portable trust without full KYC.

Recommended Differentiation

  • Solana-native Use SAS credentials/schemas/attestations instead of inventing another attestation primitive.
  • Graph-first Make reputation contextual, explainable, and composable, not a single opaque human score.
  • Privacy Publish signed score roots and support zk threshold proofs: "score >= X in context Y" without exposing the full graph.
  • Sybil Combine issuer trust, graph topology, stake/slashing, account age, activity entropy, and known human proofs from partners.
  • Developer wedge Target airdrops, DAO governance, grant programs, marketplaces, agent reputation, and undercollateralized access.
